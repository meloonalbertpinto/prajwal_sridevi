Help Center

Welcome, friend. Here are some answers to common questions about your new IoT companion.

What is the SRIDEVI (EVA) Bot?

SRIDEVI (model EVA) is your personal IoT companion bot, designed to be a friend who is there for you during your good and bad times. It uses advanced AI to listen, chat, and help you with daily tasks, providing emotional support and companionship whenever you need it.

How do I set up my bot for the first time?

Setting up your bot is simple:

  1. Place the bot on a stable, flat surface.
  2. Connect the included power adapter and turn it on.
  3. Download the 'SRIDEVI Companion' app on your smartphone.
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions in the app to connect the bot to your Wi-Fi network.
  5. Once connected, your bot is ready to chat!
How do I talk to my bot?

You have two primary ways to interact:

  • Voice: Simply say the wake word (e.g., "Hey EVA") followed by your question or comment.
  • App: Use the "ChatBot" or "Voice to Text" feature in the companion app to type or speak messages directly.
Is my data and conversation private?

Absolutely. We take your privacy very seriously. All conversations are encrypted, and your personal data is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent. You can manage your data privacy settings and history directly from the app.

How do I factory reset my bot?

If you need to reset your bot to its original factory settings, locate the small reset button on the bottom of the device. Press and hold it for 10 seconds until the bot chimes and reboots. Warning: This will erase all your custom settings and Wi-Fi configurations.
